About Me


Welcome to this website!

Design is more than making things look good or work well. It is about creating an experience that feels right and connects with people. Designers focus on building visuals that combine clear structure with a sense of atmosphere. Every colour, shape, and texture plays an important role in shaping user behaviour.

I've always been interested in how things make people feel. I love old digital design aesthetics, especially early-2000s aesthetics like Frutiger Aero. Thereâ€™s something powerful about that mix of soft gradients, luminous blues, and organic motion. It brings a sense of peace and tranquility.

Whenever I am learning design, visuals, or interactive experiences, I try to capture that same feeling it invokes. These old aesthetics carry weight and nostalgia unlike current modern aesthetics, maybe due to my own childhood memories.

This website was made to show my favorite aesthetics that make me feel a certain way. Have a look around and feel free to get in touch with me. I hope you find an aesthetic you enjoy!
